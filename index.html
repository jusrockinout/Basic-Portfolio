About Me

Evam me sutam. Yehi tathagatassa yathabhuccam vannam samma vadamana vadeyyum. Ekampi jatim dvepi jatiyo tissopi jatiyo catassopi jatiyo pancapi jatiyo dasapi jatiyo visampi jatiyo timsampi jatiyo cattalisampi jatiyo pannasampi jatiyo. Yanakatham gamakatham nigamakatham.

Yatha antava ca ayam loko ananto ca'ti. So mandatta momuhatta tattha tattha panham puttho samano vacavikkhepam apajjati amaravikkhepam. Param maranape hoti ca na ca hoti tathagato param maranape neva hoti na na hoti tathagato param maranati iticemampucchasi.

So kho, bho, atta yato kayassa bheda ucchijjati vinassati, na hoti param marana, ettavata kho, bho, ayam atta samma samucchinno hotiti. Brahmajalasuttam. Sukhanca kayena patisamvedeti, yam tam ariya acikkhanti upekkhako satima sukhavihariti. Ucchedavado.
